## Echoes of the Raven Plague Doctor

Echoes of the Raven Plague Doctor - Submission for the "Echoes of the Machine" Hytale Mod Jam

# Features
- New Legendary armor set with stats similar to the Adamantite set with a few more bonuses such as poison resistance, health regen and stamina regen. 
- The new armor set is craftable at the Armorer's Workbench.
- New "Echoes of the Raven" spell book weapon that summon ravens to fight along side you. Costing only 10 stamina each. 
- The new weapon can be crafted at the Arcanist's Workbench.
- Cog wheel on the "Echoes of the Raven" spell book weapon spins and also hovers over the book when casting.
- The Ravens you summon have green eyes.
